CURRENT SUPPORTED CHARITIES:

MEDICINE SANS FRONTIERES (DOCTORS WITHOUT BORDERS)

AN INTERNATIONAL NETWORK OF VOLUNTEERS, FIELD PROJECTS AND OFFICES DELIVERING EMERGENCY MEDICAL AID TO PEOPLE AFFECTED BY CONFLICT, EPIDEMICS, DISASTERS OR EXCLUDED FROM HEALTHCARE.

RESTLESS BEINGS

AN INTERNATIONAL HUMANITARIAN AND HUMAN RIGHTS EFFORT THAT WORKS WITH AND SUPPORTS MARGINALISED COMMUNITIES ACRSS THE WORLD THAT ARE OFTEN DEPRIVED OF MEDIA OR PUBLIC ATTENTION.

UNICEF

THE WORLD'S LEADING ORGANISATION FOR CHILDREN IN OVER 190 COUNTRIES, PROVIDING LONG-TERM HUMANITARIAN AND DEVELOPMENTAL AID TO CHILDREN AND MOTHERS IN DEVELOPING COUNTRIES ALL OVER THE WORLD.
